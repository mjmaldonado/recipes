Ingredients:
 - 2 teaspoons Pink Himalayan sea salt
 - 2 teaspoons Organic, freshly ground black pepper
 - 1 Organic, grass-fed beef filet steak, 8 - 12 ounces
 - 3 pieces thick cut, organic, pasture raised bacon
 - 1 tablespoon organic, grass-fed butter
 - 1 tablespoon coconut oil
 - 1 tablespoon organic blue cheese

Directions:
 1. Preheat the oven to 450 degrees F.
 2. Wrap the steak with the bacon and secure with a toothpick(s).
 3. Add salt and pepper to both sides of the steak.
 4. Heat an ovenproof skillet over medium-high heat.
 5. Add the butter and oil.  When melted, ad the steak to the skillet.
 6. Sear on both sides for about 1 minute per side.
 7. Remove the skillet from the stovetop and place into the oven to finish cooking.
 8. Cook until medium rare, approximately 10 minutes.
 9. Remove from the oven and sprinkle with blue cheese.
 10. Enjoy!
